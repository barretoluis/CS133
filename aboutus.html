My Career Gym focuses on helping students succeed with their careers. We are a non-profit organization and we believe that mentoring should be available for everyone without borders. Our Mentors are professional people who have worked in different companies and who have been successful with their careers. My Career Gym offers you the chance to ask professional people how they succeeded, to tell you the secrets. Our service is free and most importantly secure. All your information will be confidential and will not be used for other porpuses. My Career Gym is the place to seek advice, help and to network. Join us today. Check our services page for more information on how we can help you.
